Real Estate Broker Matching: Putting You in Control

Customize the services you need and set your commission rate. Find the right broker for your property needs.

How It Works

For Sellers and Buyers: List the services you need from brokers, such as showing a house, selling a house, buying a house, and more. Set the percentage of the commission you're willing to offer or a fixed price for each service.

For Brokers: Offer your services at competitive rates and attract a larger pool of potential clients. Make a bid, and if the seller or buyer agrees, you'll be able to exchange information and move forward with the deal.

Matching Process: Once you list your needs, the app matches you with a selection of brokers. You can review their offers and select the one that suits you best. Communication is limited until a connection is made. After a bid is accepted, parties can exchange information and proceed with the transaction.

Why Choose Us?

  • Empowering Sellers and Buyers: Take control of the commission structure and services you need. It's all about flexibility and choice.
  • Opportunities for Brokers: Access a larger pool of clients and grow your business. You only pay a small fee out of your commission when you secure a deal.
  • Secure and Confidential: Your information is kept private until you're ready to connect, ensuring a secure matching process.
