What is the Multiple Agent Service (MAS)?

An innovative platform where buyers and sellers list services and set their own commission rates. Brokers bid on these terms, allowing the buyer or seller to choose the best match.

Changing the Game: MAS replaces the outdated Multiple Listing Service (MLS) with a more flexible, customer-driven approach.

Real Estate Reimagined

BOB's MAS ensures transparent, competitive broker bidding. Buyers and Sellers can connect instantly with brokers who align with their goals, and brokers gain access to an otherwise hidden market of potential customers. We connect our users in a way that gives the reins of real estate negotiations back to the buyer and seller.
